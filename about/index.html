About BrightPath Consulting

We are a team of strategic visionaries and digital transformation experts committed to helping businesses navigate the complexities of modern commerce and achieve sustainable growth.

Our Journey of Excellence

From humble beginnings to becoming East Africa's leading consulting firm, here's how we've grown over the years.

2009

Company Founded

BrightPath Consulting was established in Nairobi with a vision to help businesses achieve strategic excellence.

2012

Digital Focus

Expanded services to include digital transformation consulting as businesses began embracing technology.

2015

Regional Expansion

Opened offices across East Africa, serving clients in Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda.

2018

Award Recognition

Received the Kenya Business Excellence Award for Outstanding Consulting Services.

2021

Global Partnerships

Formed strategic alliances with international consulting firms to serve multinational clients.

2024

Innovation Hub

Launched our innovation lab focusing on AI-driven business solutions and emerging technologies.
